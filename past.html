A Little About My Past

How Did My Interest In Computers Rise?

My interest in computers was sparked at a early age. More specifically when I was capable of playing video games. Ever since I got my first PlayStation(which was a PS3), I had a major interest in how these things worked.

This curiousity only grew larger when I was allowed to use my Dad's computer. Then Once I reached Year 8, My parents bought me my first MacBook. Since then, I've been bingeing YouTube tutorials on how to create games and applications, which is where I first learnt how to create a website.

However, I took a sudden shift in interest during Year 11, when I found out the capacity of Cyber-Security. I started playing realistic Hacking Games(They were fake), and started taking smaller short Ethical Hacking courses to learn more. That's what drove me to choose the CyberSecurity Degree here at UTS.

How was my Year 12 HSC experience?

My HSC experience was daunting and fearful as well as joyous and fun. It was a really rewarding experience which allowed me to develop as a person as well as learn new skills which would benefit me for my career and overall life-time.

Throughout my HSC experience, I made a variety of friends, and experienced a variety of experiences ranging from learning to drive to stressing crazy over an exam.

My overall HSC years were probably the best years of my life, cause I had a lot of fun, from being stupid with friends to studying real hard (I mean grinding Instagram Reels by sending my friends my entire FYP). These small experiences led to a bigger appreciation for these years within my life.
